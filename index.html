Paradox Junction
Easter Egg Guide

Full main quest walkthrough — solo and co-op. Steps ordered by consistency across multiple top community guides.

Solo Friendly No Directed Mode Wonder Weapon Required

Tips & Loadout

Recommended Weapon Novaline or any AR or LMG with range. You will have the Sundergat for mechanics, so bring a ranged weapon as your backup for the boss.
Must-Have Perks Jugger-Nog and Stamin-Up early. You need Death Perception to find the Headset and Wisp Tea to retrieve the Goggles. Both are required.
Gobble Gums Shields Up and Aftertaste are both blue rarity — easy to stock. Kazmirs are excellent for boss concentration field defenses. Monkey Bombs help throughout.
Two Versions of the Map You play in Destroyed (post-nuke future) and Past (pre-nuke). Dog rounds auto-teleport you. The Temporal Conduit costs 1,500 Essence to switch manually.
Solo Tip For tortured zombie steps and children's games, thin the active round down to 1 or 2 zombies before triggering them. Far easier to manage solo.
Equipment to Bring Tomahawks or any explosive, Molotovs, and a Psych or Stun Grenade. All can be purchased at the crafting bench if needed.

Prerequisites

As soon as you spawn, head to the Yellow House backyard and hold interact on the body stabbed with a golf club to collect the Truck Keys.

Also in the Yellow House, go upstairs and interact with the chemistry set on the desk to collect a vial of SO3. Then cross to the Green House kitchen and interact with the sink to fill it with acid. This gives you H2SO4, needed later for a Blundergat part.

Do This Round 1Both can be grabbed immediately. Doing the acid early saves backtracking later.

Survive until Round 5 or 6. Completing the dog round automatically teleports you to Past Nuke Town.

In the Past, bring your Truck Keys to the truck and hold interact to move it. This opens the Trinity Avenue section of the map.

Shoot the four Space-Time Knots floating around the Pack-a-Punch. Each shot spawns purple-eyed zombies tethered to that knot — kill them all to destroy it. Repeat for all four.

Pack-a-Punch Is Now LiveUpgrade your weapons whenever you have the Essence. You will need upgraded weapons for the rest of the egg.

Build the Blundergat

All four parts can be collected in any order. The humming cyst and mannequin can sometimes be found before Pack-a-Punch if you get lucky early.

Walk around Destroyed Nuke Town listening for a yellow Loot Cyst making a special humming or ringing sound. When you find it, kill zombies near it to feed it souls. Once full, it drops the Barrel.

Can't Find It?It may be in Trinity Avenue, which opens after Pack-a-Punch. Fill all other cysts anyway — they drop useful loot regardless.

In Destroyed, walk the map and listen for a mannequin making the same humming sound. Check the Yellow House, Green House, and Trinity Avenue. Remember its location — do not interact yet.

Switch to Past Nuke Town, go to that same mannequin, and hold interact to pour the acid vial on its head. A twin quote confirms the correct mannequin.

Return to Destroyed — the Hammer will be on the floor at that spot.

Wrong Mannequin?You get three attempts. Refill the vial at the Green House sink and try another mannequin.

In Destroyed, listen for a wall with black goo making the ringing sound. It spawns in one of three spots:

  • Top of the stairs in the Green House, right side
  • Inside the Yellow House garage
  • Trinity Avenue corridor on the right side when entering

Memorize the spot, then go to Past and throw an explosive at that exact wall location to blow it open.

Return to Destroyed — the wall now has a hole. Interact with it to collect the Stock.

Go to Past Nuke Town. Head upstairs in the Yellow House to the fourth room with a large window. Pick up the Sealant from the bottom-left corner of the shelf.

Easiest PartFixed location — always in the same spot. Grab it any time after Pack-a-Punch is unlocked.

Go to Destroyed. Find the truck in the center of the Cul-de-Sac and head inside to the workbench.

Melee the crafting bench first to open the flap, then hold interact to place your parts and craft the Blundergat.

Melee the Bench First — This Is RequiredThe bench will not show an interact prompt until after you melee it. This catches many players off guard.

Upgrade to Sundergat

Spend a full round in Destroyed. At the start of the next round, a Tortured Zombie spawns inside a fire tornado in the Yellow House backyard, Green House backyard, or Trinity Avenue.

Walk near it to release it. It will try to tether to nearby zombies using a fire chain that drains its health. Kill any purple-eyed zombies it tethers to — keep the main Tortured Zombie alive the entire time.

Lead it to the crafting bench in the truck and kill it with the Blundergat right in front of the bench. Its soul is absorbed. Repeat for a second Tortured Zombie the next round, then a Shock Mimic on the third round (same process, slightly more health).

Must Use the BlundergatAny other weapon will not count. The kill must happen directly in front of the bench.
Solo TipThin the round to 1 or 2 zombies before approaching the tornado. Fewer zombies to tether to.

After all three souls are absorbed, travel to Past Nuke Town. Go to the crafting bench, melee it open, and place your Blundergat inside.

Return to Destroyed, melee the bench open again, and pick up your Sundergat.

Pack-a-Punch It NowThe Sundergat is required for all remaining escort steps — Sundergat kills are the only way to move soul boxes toward their destinations.

Summon the Twins

Find the RC-XD remote in one of three spots in Destroyed:

  • Yellow House garage — bottom or broken shelf
  • Green House backyard — back corner of the soil pile
  • Trinity Avenue — near the crates beside Pack-a-Punch

Holding interact starts the RC car mini-game. Drive this path: up the first ramp, across, up the second ramp, jump over the fence, through the gap, then detonate beside the barrel near the garage door on Trinity Avenue.

The garage opens. Head inside, interact with the kneeling body to make it fade (pick up the head on the safe), then wait a moment for Chalk to appear on the ground.

RC Car TipsKeep steady momentum before the ramp and jump early. If you fail, find another controller next round. It may not spawn until Round 8.

In Past, go to the swing set in the Yellow House backyard. Melee or shoot the swing seat to knock it off. Pick it up from the ground.

Return to Destroyed and interact with the swing set to replace the seat. Then hold interact again to place the Chalk on top of the swing set.

The Twins appear in a short sequence. Once their dialogue ends, all remaining Easter egg steps are unlocked.

After This PointIrradiated Seeds become available in the Yellow House garage, and the Piano Teacher can begin spawning. Steps 12 through 15 can be done in any order.

In Destroyed, go to the left of Pack-a-Punch and find the wooden telephone pole or speaker pylon. Shoot the very top of it until the Goggles fall off outside the map boundary.

Purchase the Wisp Tea perk (perk locations are randomized — check both versions or use Wonderfizz). Once your Wisp is active, walk near the fallen Goggles and your Wisp automatically carries them into the play area for you to pick up.

Wisp Tea Is RequiredYou cannot pick up the Goggles yourself. Buy the perk before attempting this step.

You must have Death Perception equipped to see and pick up the Headset. Buy it from a perk machine or Wonderfizz, then check these locations in Destroyed:

  • Yellow House backyard — in the mannequin's hand near the perk machine spawn
  • Green House second floor — on the wall hooks or rack
  • Near the garbage can by the fence in the Cul-de-Sac

In Past Nuke Town, find the Toy Box at the entrance of Trinity Avenue. Hold interact to open it, then place both the Goggles and the Headset inside.

Unlocks the Four Square BallThe red ball for Four Square will now appear in Destroyed Nuke Town, needed for Step 17.

Plant the Tree & Light the Fire

Get Seeds: In Destroyed, go to the Yellow House garage and crouch in front of the red box on the shelf. Hold interact to collect the Irradiated Seeds. Only available after Step 11.

Plant the Tree: In Past, find the soil pit to the left of Pack-a-Punch in Trinity Avenue. Hold interact to plant. A glowing green plant appears.

Feed Souls: Kill zombies near the plant with the Blundergat or Sundergat to feed it roughly 50 souls. Your screen flashes white when complete.

Chop the Tree: In Destroyed, the tree is now enormous. Throw 3 Tomahawks at it to get 3 pieces of Strange Firewood. Buy Tomahawks at the crafting bench or shoot purple crystals to find them.

Light the Fire: In Past, place the firewood in the Yellow House fireplace on the first floor. Throw a Molotov to ignite it. A completion quote confirms success.

After This StepA white X appears in Trinity Avenue in Destroyed. Standing on it triggers Hopscotch in Step 16.

Children's Games

Three games: Hopscotch, Four Square, and Jacks. They can be worked on across rounds in any order. Each soul box requires Sundergat kills to escort.

In Destroyed, find the white X on the ground in Trinity Avenue near Pack-a-Punch. Stand on it until it glows blue and stays lit after you step away. Then immediately run to the Temporal Conduit and teleport to Past.

In Past, the Twins draw a Hopscotch grid in the Trinity Avenue west area. Interact with the orb at square 1 to enter third-person mode and begin.

How to play: Jump from square 1 through 12 in order, then back from 12 to 1. A black square moves along the tiles — never land on it. Take a half-second pause between each jump. Accuracy matters more than speed.

Black square tip: Watch it cross squares 6 and 7, then time your jump to hop over it. Follow behind it on the return trip from 12.

Failed?Wait about 60 seconds and interact to retry. The grid will reset.

Success spawns a glowing gold Music Box. Get kills with the Sundergat near it to escort it. It travels through the Yellow House backyard and kitchen before landing on the Yellow House fireplace. A Max Ammo drop confirms completion.

Step 14 must be complete before this step.

In Destroyed, find the red ball — it phases in and out of visibility, so look carefully — in one of three spots:

  • On top of the Yellow House roof on the Trinity Avenue side
  • On top of stacked ammo crates or wooden boxes in Trinity Avenue
  • In the Yellow House backyard on top of a pillar

Shoot the ball down, then push it with a bullet weapon all the way to the white X in the Green House backyard. The X glows when the ball is in place.

Teleport to Past. The Twins draw a Four Square grid. Interact with the ball to start. Melee the ball into an adjacent square — it must bounce into a different square each time. Never let it bounce in the same square twice.

Solo: Run to the opposite square after each hit. Co-op: Position a teammate to melee it back. Solo takes about 15 seconds; co-op about 30.

Failed?Wait about 60 seconds and retry. Ether Shroud is great for keeping zombies off you during this game.

Success spawns a soul box. Escort it with Sundergat kills to the Toy Box in Trinity Avenue. Max Ammo confirms completion.

Piano Teacher & Symbol Puzzle

After Step 11, a special Piano Teacher zombie can spawn in Destroyed. Hang around the Green House backyard — she may appear near the back-left archway or wander out into the yard. She moves slowly and looks different from regular zombies. Wait up to two full minutes.

Do Not Kill HerShe must reach the piano alive. If she does not spawn, go to the next round and stay in the Green House backyard for the full round.

Once she spawns, throw a Psych Grenade at her (or use Brain Rot on your weapon). She will run into the Green House and stand at the piano.

While she is at the piano, immediately teleport to Past. Go to the Green House — she will be at the piano there too. She plays briefly, then explodes. The piano glows yellow, confirming success.

Unlocks the 8 Musical SymbolsBlue symbol notes now appear around Destroyed Nuke Town. Required for the piano step and for Jacks to become available.

In Destroyed, find all 8 glowing blue symbols. Count how many times each blinks before pausing — do not interact yet. Write them down. Then interact with them starting with the one that blinks once, then twice, all the way up to the one that blinks eight times.

All 8 possible spawn locations:

  • Back fence of the Yellow House backyard
  • Side of the school bus in the Cul-de-Sac
  • Back of the bunker in the Green House backyard
  • Main Nuke Town center fence, facing east
  • Trinity Avenue left side, near the mailbox
  • Trinity Avenue right side corridor
  • Trinity Avenue further right side, near a front door
  • Trinity Avenue far back, behind the rock near Pack-a-Punch
Cannot Interact With Symbols?You have not finished feeding the tree in Step 15. Get more Sundergat kills near the plant until the screen flashes white, then return.
Made a Mistake?Go to the next round — the symbols reset for another attempt.

Go to Past Nuke Town and find the piano on the ground floor of the Green House. Interact with it and play these 8 notes in this exact order — they are the same in every game:

A — F — G — E — F — E — C — E

The keys are labeled 1 through 8 on screen when you interact. Follow them in order.

Wrong Notes?Interact again after about 30 seconds and retry.
Activates Floating ZombiesSuccess triggers zombies to float outside the map boundary in Destroyed — you need these for the Jacks game in Step 21.

Jacks

In Destroyed, look around the outer edges of the map for zombies floating sideways in the air outside the perimeter. Shoot 3 of them — their souls fly toward a new white X in Trinity Avenue.

Teleport to Past and interact with the orb at the X to start Jacks.

How to play: A ball bounces and launches zombies into the air with each bounce. Kill all airborne zombies before the ball hits the ground. They die in one hit. Aim quickly and line up shots. Repeat for 4 to 6 waves.

No Explosives Near the BallAny explosive hit to the ball immediately fails the step. Use the Sundergat or a bullet weapon only.
Failed?Wait 60 seconds and interact to retry.

Success spawns a Musical Sheet soul box. Escort it with Sundergat kills — it travels through the Green House and ends at the Green House piano. Screen flashes white on completion.

The Clock & Boss Trigger

Get to a high position in Destroyed to access the large clock on the roof of the Green House. Shoot the white hand to the 12 o'clock position, then shoot the red hand to 12 o'clock as well. Both must be at zero.

Now: Activate the teleport to Past. During the animation, before it completes, shoot the red hand one more time — moving it just past 12 o'clock.

If timed correctly, you arrive in Past with a sound cue. A soul orb appears — follow it to the piano, the toy box, the fireplace, and finally to the swing set in the Yellow House backyard where it glows yellow.

Last Prep Before BossAll three soul box escorts (Steps 16, 17, 21) must be done. Pack-a-Punch tier three. Full armor. Best gobble gums equipped. Boss fight is next.

In Past, follow the blue orbs as the Twins take you to three locations. You only need to follow and listen — no actions required.

The final stop is the swing set in the Yellow House backyard. Interact with the yellow orb to vote to start the boss fight.

Point of No ReturnOnce you vote, the boss fight begins and you cannot go back. Triple Pack-a-Punch your weapons, fill all armor plates, and have Kazmirs, Monkey Bombs, and Ether Shroud charges ready.

Boss Fight — The Dark Heart

Three active locations throughout all phases: Trinity Avenue, Yellow House Backyard, and Green House Backyard.

Cyst Mechanic

Yellow Cysts guard black blobs at each of the three locations. Kill 8 to 10 zombies near each cyst to fill it with souls. When full, the blob is destroyed and an artifact is revealed. Do this at all three locations.

Concentration Field Defense

A blue field spawns at one artifact location. Defend it from zombies — if it reaches zero health, the defense restarts. Use Kazmirs, Monkey Bombs, or the Blundergat. The orb moves to the next location when done. Follow it for two more defenses — three total.

Damage Phase

The Dark Heart is now vulnerable. Shoot only its bright glowing spots — everything else is immune. Reduce it to about one-third health to trigger Phase 2.

Orb Mechanic

Yellow orbs shoot down zombies tethered to the black blobs. Shoot each orb to spawn those zombies, then kill them to destroy the blob. Same as the Pack-a-Punch step. Repeat at all three locations.

New Hazard — Meteors and Fire Tornadoes

Orange circles on the ground mark incoming meteor strikes — dodge them. Fire tornadoes drain your armor and slow your movement rapidly. They are more dangerous than the meteors. Keep moving at all times.

Concentration Fields and Damage

Three more defenses, same pattern as Phase 1 but Rad-Hounds join in. You receive Max Armor and Max Ammo after each health-bar third. The boss moves faster this phase — lead your shots.

Tortured Zombie Mechanic

A Tortured Zombie spawns in the center. Clear nearby zombies, then escort it toward a black blob. Watch for a yellow tether connecting it to the blob. Once the tether appears, shoot the Tortured Zombie to destroy the blob. It respawns — repeat at all three locations.

Do Not Kill It Before It TethersThe Tortured Zombie must be actively tethered to the blob before you shoot it. Killing it early is a significant setback.
Final Concentration Fields and Damage

Three final defenses. Then the Dark Heart spins rapidly, making weak spots hard to track. Use all remaining equipment. The Blundergat deals excellent damage but requires accurate aim. Keep moving — fire tornadoes and meteors are at their most dangerous here.

Boss Defeated — Easter Egg CompleteThe ending cutscene plays. Rewards: Main Quest Complete Calling Card and a unique Blundergat weapon skin.
